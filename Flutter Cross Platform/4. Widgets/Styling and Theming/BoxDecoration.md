> Widget used for Styling the Container
### Fields:
- padding:
- alignment: control the position of the child of this container
- shape: `BoxShape.circle`
- color:
- image: [[DecorationImage]]
- `borderRadius`: making curves in the container or even turn it into circle
- border: `Border.all( )`   
	- color: controls the color of the edges of the container 
	- width:  the thickness of the edge
- `boxShadow`:  list of [[BoxShadow]]
- gradient: [[LinearGradient]] / RadialGradient / SweepGradient
